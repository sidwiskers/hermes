# Code of conduct

Hermes is built in public by people with different backgrounds and levels of
experience. Treat technical disagreement as collaboration, not conflict.

## Expected behavior

- Be precise, respectful, and patient.
- Critique code and ideas rather than people.
- Make space for questions and acknowledge corrections openly.
- Protect private information and disclose security concerns responsibly.

Harassment, threats, discrimination, deliberate humiliation, unwanted sexual
attention, doxxing, and sustained disruption are not acceptable.

## Scope and enforcement

This policy applies in project repositories, reviews, issues, discussions, and
other spaces where someone represents Hermes. Maintainers may edit or remove
content, restrict participation, or ban contributors when necessary to protect
the community.

Report conduct concerns privately through the repository's security-advisory
form and prefix the report title with `[conduct]`. Reports will be handled
confidentially and without retaliation. Include links or screenshots when they
are needed to understand the incident, but do not unnecessarily repeat harmful
content.

This policy is informed by the Contributor Covenant 2.1.
